Key Insights & Competitive Intelligence

1. Janssen/SPRAVATO Dominates Florida Psychiatry KOL Spending

Janssen Pharmaceuticals (Johnson & Johnson) is by far the largest pharma spender on FL psychiatrist speakers. SPRAVATO (esketamine nasal spray for treatment-resistant depression) accounts for an estimated $450,000+ in Florida psychiatrist speaker fees across 2021-2024. At least 15 unique FL psychiatrists have been paid to speak about SPRAVATO.

2. David Sheehan Is Florida's Highest-Paid Psychiatrist KOL

Dr. David Sheehan (Tampa/Lutz) received $94,776 in a single 2022 payment from Janssen R&D, plus additional consulting, speaker fees, and royalties. His total across 2021-2024 exceeds $118,000. He is likely involved in SPRAVATO clinical research/development beyond just speaking.

3. Todd Broder Is the Most Prolific SPRAVATO Speaker

Dr. Todd Broder (Forensic Psychiatry, Gainesville) has the highest volume of individual speaking engagements across all 4 years, with 40+ individual payment records from Janssen totaling $115K+. He speaks almost exclusively on SPRAVATO.

4. TMS Speaking Is a Growing Category

Neuronetics (NeuroStar TMS) has built a Florida speaker bureau led by Dr. Kenneth Pages (Tampa, $30K+) and Dr. Lindsay Israel-Gaines (West Palm Beach, $8.5K+). This represents a device company, not pharma, and focuses on TMS therapy for depression.

5. Geographic Concentration: Tampa & Gainesville

Tampa Bay and Gainesville (UF Health) are the primary hubs for psychiatrist pharma speakers in Florida. The Miami-Dade/Broward area has more individual psychiatrists receiving payments but at generally lower amounts.

6. Emerging Drug Categories

  • COBENFY (BMS) - Novel schizophrenia treatment with muscarinic mechanism. Only 1 FL psychiatrist (Kakar) found receiving consulting fees so far (2024). Expected to grow significantly.
  • VRAYLAR (AbbVie) - Growing FL speaker presence (Aly, Cutler) for bipolar/schizophrenia/MDD adjunct.
  • Azstarys (Corium) - ADHD medication with 5+ FL psychiatrist speakers, led by Cutler and Perez.
