Section 6: Clinical Insight of the Day

COMP360 Psilocybin Phase 3 Results: What Clinicians Need to Know About Dosing in TRD

Evidence Update TRD Psilocybin

The Pearl: In the COMP006 Phase 3 trial (N=581), two fixed doses of COMP360 psilocybin 25 mg administered 3 weeks apart produced a statistically significant (p<0.001) and clinically meaningful (-3.8 point) reduction in MADRS scores versus 1 mg at Week 6. Notably, a 10 mg dose arm was also included (n=142), providing granularity on dose-response. Across both Phase 3 trials (COMP005 and COMP006), adverse events were mild to moderate and mostly resolved within 24 hours.

Clinical significance: The two-dose, three-weeks-apart paradigm is clinically practical and different from single-dose psychedelic protocols. The 26-week durability data (expected Q3 2026) will be critical for clinical decision-making. Importantly, psilocybin therapy requires a therapeutic support framework — not just a prescription — which opens opportunities for multi-disciplinary practice models like Total Mental Wellness.

Three ways to use this:

  • Talking point for pharma meetings: "The COMP006 data confirms a practical two-dose protocol for TRD with a favorable safety profile. The question for community psychiatrists is how we build the infrastructure for supervised psychedelic-assisted therapy in outpatient settings — something my multi-provider practice is uniquely positioned to discuss."
  • LinkedIn post script: "Two Phase 3 wins for psilocybin in TRD. But the real conversation isn't just about efficacy data — it's about how we deliver this safely in real-world practice. As a community psychiatrist running a multi-location practice, here's how I'm thinking about integrating novel therapies..."
  • CME lecture foundation: "From Esketamine to Psilocybin: Building a Practical Treatment Algorithm for TRD in 2026" — Submit this as an abstract for Psych Congress or ASCP, or pitch it to NEI/PsychU as a webinar.
