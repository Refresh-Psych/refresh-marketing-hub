## KEY OBSERVATIONS

### Drug/Product Trends Across 2018-2020
1. **2018**: Dominated by **Janssen (INVEGA TRINZA/SUSTENNA)** - long-acting injectable antipsychotics for schizophrenia. Also Shire (MYDAYIS/VYVANSE) for ADHD.
2. **2019**: Major shift to **Avanir (NUEDEXTA)** for pseudobulbar affect - many of the highest individual-year totals. Also Janssen launching **SPRAVATO** (esketamine) speaker programs. Sumitomo Dainippon **Latuda** active in NY.
3. **2020**: COVID impact reduced overall volume. Shift to **Janssen (SPRAVATO)** as dominant speaker program. **Allergan (VRAYLAR)** emerging. **LivaNova (VNS Therapy)** appearing for treatment-resistant depression.

### Manufacturer Market Share (Speaker Programs)
- **Janssen Pharmaceuticals**: Present in all 3 years, all 10 states. Products: INVEGA TRINZA, INVEGA SUSTENNA, SPRAVATO
- **Avanir Pharmaceuticals**: Dominant in 2019 with NUEDEXTA, reduced in 2020
- **Shire/Takeda**: Active 2018-2019 with MYDAYIS and VYVANSE (ADHD)
- **Allergan**: Emerging 2020 with VRAYLAR (cariprazine)
- **LivaNova USA**: 2020 with VNS Therapy
- **Sumitomo Dainippon Pharma**: 2019 with Latuda (lurasidone)
- **Lilly USA**: Sporadic high-value individual payments
