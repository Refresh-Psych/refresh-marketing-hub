7. Notable Trends in Psychiatric Drug Marketing

1
Extreme Payment Concentration
The top 1% of psychiatrists (570 of 56,955) receive 74.7% of all industry payments. These KOLs function as force multipliers, influencing prescribing through lectures, publications, and guideline authorship.
2
Shift to Antipsychotics & Specialty Drugs
Highest spending has moved from traditional antidepressants (now generic) to branded atypical antipsychotics (Vraylar, Rexulti, Caplyta), LAIs (Invega, Abilify Maintena), and novel mechanisms (Spravato, VMAT2 inhibitors). SGAs captured 61% of antipsychotic market revenue in 2023.
3
Spravato: Fastest-Growing Speaker Program
89% revenue growth in 2023. J&J investing heavily in KOL programs to normalize REMS-certified, in-office esketamine for TRD. Peak sales forecast: $1.5B+.
4
Tardive Dyskinesia: A Marketing-Created $3B Market
Ingrezza + Austedo = $3B+ revenue for a historically under-treated condition. Both companies invested in "disease awareness" speaker programs. Nearly 50% of prescribers received payments. Textbook pharma market expansion.
5
Geographic Concentration: FL, TX, OH, OK
Top 1% of paid psychiatrists are disproportionately in Florida, Texas, Ohio, and Oklahoma. May reflect higher speaker activity, permissive environments, or targeted strategy in large Medicaid/Medicare states.
6
CME as Shadow Speaker Program
Pharma funds CME companies that "independently" select speakers. These payments don't appear in Open Payments, creating significant undercounting. KOLs like Stahl (NEI) and Masand (Global Medical Education) operate at this intersection.
7
Medicare Price Negotiation Impact
Vraylar selected for Medicare negotiation under IRA. May compress margins and redirect marketing budgets. Companies may increase KOL activity to protect share before negotiated prices take effect.
8
LAI Antipsychotic Push
Invega, Abilify Maintena/Asimtufii, Aristada, Uzedy represent the fastest-growing schizophrenia segment. Speaker programs emphasize adherence benefits. J&J's Invega franchise alone = $2.9B.
9
Payments Correlate with Prescribing
Peer-reviewed evidence: physicians receiving payments prescribe more branded drugs. For VMAT2 inhibitors, the correlation is statistically significant (p < 0.001). States with top-quartile ad spend see +11% stimulants, +13% antidepressants, +10% antipsychotics.
10
New Entrants: PPD & Psychedelic-Adjacent
Zurzuvae ($15,900/course) launched Dec 2023 as first oral PPD treatment. Sage/Biogen building reproductive psychiatry KOL networks. Spravato's success validates "psychedelic-adjacent" commercial models.
