  • Statute of limitations: 6 years from last date of treatment
  • Board complaints become public 10 days after filing (even before probable cause determination)
  • Insurance costs: $12,000-$85,000/year depending on specialty, claims history, coverage limits
  • Critical: Verify malpractice policy explicitly covers telepsychiatry services. Not all carriers do automatically.

Standard of Care in Telepsychiatry — Key Principles

  • Same standard as in-person care — no lower bar for telehealth encounters
  • Standard determined at patient location — local (not provider) standard applies
  • Documentation must note medium limitations and steps taken to minimize them
  • Informed consent must cover: telehealth limitations, tech failure contingencies, emergency protocols, HIPAA/data security
  • Emergency protocols must be pre-established for each patient's geographic location
