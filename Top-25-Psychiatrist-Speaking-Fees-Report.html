Executive Summary

This report analyzes CMS Open Payments data (2021-2024) covering pharmaceutical industry payments to psychiatrists nationwide. The psychiatric speaker fee landscape is extremely top-heavy: just 2.8% of psychiatrists capture 82.6% of all industry payments. The top 50 national speakers average $629,980 in 5-year speaking fees ($125,996/yr), while the median psychiatrist receives just $194/year. This report includes both a national Top 50 ranking and a dedicated Florida Top 50 ranking with CMS API-verified data, plus a detailed comparison showing where Dr. Audrey La Noce, DO ranks in both landscapes.

  10. Target advisory boards (higher per-event rate). Advisory board compensation ($3,000-$10,000/meeting) exceeds speaker fees. Most top-25 speakers participate in 2-4 advisory boards annually, adding $20-40K/year.

National Speaker Tier Analysis

  • Tier 1 — Elite KOLs ($500K+/yr, Ranks 1-5): Multi-decade careers, 5+ pharma relationships, national/international reputation, textbook authors, conference keynotes. Example: Stephen Stahl (30+ years, author of Stahl's Essential Psychopharmacology).
  • Tier 2 — Established Speakers ($100K-$500K/yr, Ranks 6-20): 10+ years speaking, 3-5 pharma companies, regional-to-national recognition, consistent event volume. Example: Craig Chepke, Roueen Rafeyan.
  • Tier 3 — Rising Speakers ($35K-$100K/yr, Ranks 21-50): 3-8 years active, 1-3 companies, building reputation, increasing event volume. Dr. La Noce is at the top of this tier.
  • Tier 4 — Emerging ($5K-$35K/yr, Top 3-10%): New to bureaus, single-company, building track record. Where Dr. La Noce was in 2021-2023.
  • Tier 5 — Occasional ($194/yr median): The vast majority of psychiatrists who receive only food/beverage from drug reps.
