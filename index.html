Provider credentials and background. Office environment and team photos (no patient info visible). Responses to questions that redirect to booking without giving individualized advice.

Responding to DMs that appear to describe clinical situations (redirect: "This sounds important — please book a consultation").
🚫 Never Do: Confirm or deny someone is a patient. Reference any specific patient's condition, medications, or appointments in any post or reply. Use real patient names or photos without explicit HIPAA-compliant authorization. Engage in therapeutic relationships via DMs or comments.
