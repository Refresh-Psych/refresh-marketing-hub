The 2026 experts are blunt: effective staffing models rely on clinicians “adept at supervision, delegation, and team leadership.” When you scale, your senior psychiatrist’s most valuable skill stops being diagnosis and becomes supervising NPs — but almost no one interviews for it. Ask: “Tell me about a time you corrected another provider’s clinical decision — what did you say, and what was the relationship like afterward?” A great answer beats a perfect board score for a growing practice.

Output: a one-page staffing model that names which seats are core and which are flex for each state.
