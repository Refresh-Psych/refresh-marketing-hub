Action Items

This Week
1. This weekend, sign up for a free trial of DeepCura ($129/mo, best overall for psychiatry) or Freed AI ($99/mo, easiest setup). Use it in your next patient session on Monday. The published ROI: AI scribes save 1–2 hours of documentation per day, deliver a 387% first-year ROI, and allow psychiatrists to see 2–3 additional patients weekly — generating $20K–$30K annually on a $129/month subscription. The only rule: get a signed HIPAA BAA from the vendor before using it clinically.
2. Conduct a BAA audit of your AI tool stack. Every AI tool that touches patient data — EHR, telehealth platform, AI scribe, scheduling software, billing — must have a signed Business Associate Agreement on file. Standard ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini do NOT sign BAAs and are HIPAA non-compliant for patient data. If you are currently using any general-purpose AI tool with patient information, stop immediately and switch to a HIPAA-compliant alternative. This is the #1 compliance gap in telepsychiatry practices in 2026.
3. Add a patient location verification field to your telehealth intake workflow this week. Before every session, your intake process must ask: "What state will you physically be located in during this appointment?" This is both a DEA compliance requirement and a CMS billing requirement — claims must reflect the patient’s physical location. For Refresh’s 16 locations across FL, MA, and TX, this single workflow change eliminates the most common telehealth billing error and multi-state licensing risk.
4. Set up an AI-driven no-show prevention sequence in your EHR or scheduling platform. Target sequence: 72-hour email confirmation + 48-hour text reply-to-confirm + 2-hour telehealth link text + 15-minute join reminder. Tools available natively in Athena, SimplePractice, and DrChrono, or through Luma Health or Relatient. Industry no-show rate in telepsychiatry is 18–22%. This sequence cuts it by 40–60%. On 20 patients per day at $250 per session, that recovers $9,000–$13,750 in monthly revenue.
5. Implement a 90-minute Friday AI content sprint starting this week. Use Claude or ChatGPT (without any patient data) to draft: 2 SEO blog posts, 5 social media captions, and 1 email newsletter. Review for clinical accuracy, then schedule via Buffer or Hootsuite. This workflow keeps all 16 Refresh locations content-fed across every channel with 90 minutes of weekly input. Talkiatry’s $210M raise is partly funding their content and AI marketing engine. This is how a 16-location independent practice matches it without the capital.
